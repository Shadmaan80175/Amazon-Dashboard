## Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** designed to analyze Amazon sales data and uncover actionable business insights.  
This project focuses on **sales performance, regional trends, product analysis, and order status tracking** to support data-driven decision-making in e-commerce.

[Amazon Dashboard Demo.webm](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/780ef2b1-e4ed-4df5-b045-7bdb29cb2f5b)


---

##  Project Overview :

The **Amazon Sales Analytics Dashboard** provides a consolidated view of sales data across products, cities, and states.  
It helps identify top-performing regions, understand sales trends over time, and monitor order fulfillment status.

---

##  Key Features :

-  **Overall Sales Metrics**
  - Total Sales Amount
  - Total Sales Units
  - Seller Count

-  **Geographical Analysis**
  - Sales by State
  - Sales by City

-  **Product Analysis**
  - Category-wise product performance
  - Individual product sales and revenue

-  **Time-Based Trends**
  - Monthly sales trends
  - Units sold over time

-  **Order Status Tracking**
  - Shipped
  - Delivered
  - Returned
  - Pending / Cancelled

-  **Interactive Filters**
  - Product Category
  - Order Status
  - Date Range
